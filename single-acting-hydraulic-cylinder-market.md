<p><p>Single acting hydraulic cylinders are essential components in various industrial applications. The four main types include tie rod, welded, telescopic, and mill type cylinders. Tie rod cylinders are compact and cost-effective, ideal for light to medium duty applications. Welded cylinders are robust and durable, suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Telescopic cylinders offer increasing and decreasing strokes, making them versatile for different applications. Mill type cylinders are specially designed for high-pressure, heavy-duty applications in industries such as steel mills and foundries.</p></p>
