<p><p>Structural sealants market can be classified into different types based on the material composition. Silicon, polysulfide (PS), polyurethane (PU), acrylic, and other types of structural sealants are widely used in construction and automotive industries for bonding and sealing applications. Silicon sealants are known for their weather resistance and durability, while PS sealants offer excellent adhesion on various surfaces. PU sealants are flexible and provide strong bonds, while acrylic sealants are easy to apply and paintable. Other types include butyl, polysiloxane, and hybrid sealants.</p></p>
