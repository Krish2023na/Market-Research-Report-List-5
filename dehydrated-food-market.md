<p><p>The dehydrated food market can be segmented into various types based on the drying method used. These include Spray Dried, Freeze Dried, Vacuum Dried, Sun Dried, Hot Air Dried, and Others. Spray drying involves spraying a liquid form of the food into a hot chamber to remove moisture. Freeze drying involves freezing the food and then removing the ice through sublimation. Vacuum drying involves removing moisture in a low-pressure environment. Sun drying utilizes the sun's heat to remove moisture. Hot air drying involves exposing the food to hot air to remove moisture. Others include methods such as drum drying, infrared drying, and microwave drying.</p></p>
