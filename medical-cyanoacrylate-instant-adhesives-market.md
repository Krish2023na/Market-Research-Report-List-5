<p><p>Medical cyanoacrylate instant adhesives are available in three main viscosity types: under 100 cps, 100-1000 cps, and above 1000 cps. Adhesives with viscosity under 100 cps are ideal for applications requiring quick bonding with minimal gap filling. Those with viscosity between 100-1000 cps offer a balance between fast bonding and gap filling capabilities. Adhesives with viscosity above 1000 cps are best suited for applications requiring strong bond strength and excellent gap filling properties. Each viscosity type caters to specific requirements in the medical industry.</p></p>
