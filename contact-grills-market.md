<p><p>Contact grills come in two main types: Liquid Propane (LP) Grills and Natural Gas (NG) Grills. LP grills use propane gas stored in portable tanks, providing flexibility in placement and easy transportation for outdoor cooking. On the other hand, NG grills are connected to a natural gas line, offering a continuous fuel source for consistent cooking. Both types of grills offer efficient heat distribution, easy temperature control, and quick cooking times, making them popular choices for outdoor grilling enthusiasts.</p></p>
