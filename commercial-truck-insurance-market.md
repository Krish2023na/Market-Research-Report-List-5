<p><strong>The Commercial Truck Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compulsory Insurance</li><li>Optional Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compulsory insurance in the commercial truck insurance market refers to coverage that is required by law, such as liability insurance to protect against damages or injuries caused by the truck. Optional insurance, on the other hand, refers to additional coverage that truck owners can choose to purchase for extra protection, such as comprehensive coverage for damages not related to collisions or additional liability coverage. Both types of insurance play a crucial role in ensuring financial protection for truck owners in the event of accidents or other unforeseen events.</p></p>
