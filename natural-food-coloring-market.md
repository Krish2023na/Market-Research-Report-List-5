<p><p>Natural food coloring market types include Caramel Color, Capsanthin, Turmeric, Carotenoids, Annatto, Carmine, Red Beet, Spirulina, Chlorophyll, and others. Caramel Color is derived from caramelized sugar and ranges from yellow to brown. Capsanthin is extracted from red peppers and imparts a red hue. Turmeric provides a yellow color and has antioxidant properties. Carotenoids come from plants and are responsible for orange hues. Annatto is derived from the seeds of the Achiote tree and gives a yellow to orange color. Carmine is made from crushed bugs and provides a red shade. Red Beet comes from beets and gives a red color. Spirulina is a blue-green algae and provides a blue-green color. Chlorophyll is derived from plants and gives a green shade. Others in the market include colors from sources like purple sweet potatoes and saffron.</p></p>
