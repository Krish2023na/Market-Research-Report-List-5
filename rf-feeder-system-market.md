<p><p>RF Feeder System market includes two main types: Coaxial Cable and Open Wire or Twin Feeder. Coaxial cable is a type of cable that consists of an inner conductor surrounded by a tubular insulating layer, which is then covered by a conducting shield. This design helps to minimize signal loss and interference. On the other hand, Open Wire or Twin Feeder consists of two parallel wires used to carry RF signals. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
