<p><p>Multi-Mode Fiber Distributed Temperature Sensing technology is available in two market types - Single-end Configuration and Loop Configuration. In Single-end Configuration, the fiber optic cable is connected to a single end, making it suitable for point-to-point temperature monitoring. On the other hand, Loop Configuration involves connecting both ends of the fiber optic cable, creating a loop for continuous temperature monitoring along the entire length. Both configurations offer unique advantages and are utilized in various industries for precision temperature measurement and monitoring purposes.</p></p>
