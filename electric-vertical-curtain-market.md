<p><p>The Electric Vertical Curtain market offers two main types of control systems: Infrared Remote Control and Radio Remote Control. Infrared Remote Control operates by sending signals through infrared light to control the curtains, requiring a direct line of sight between the remote and the sensor. On the other hand, Radio Remote Control uses radio waves to operate the curtains, allowing for greater flexibility and distance between the remote and the sensor. Both types of control systems offer convenient ways to adjust and operate electric vertical curtains.</p></p>
