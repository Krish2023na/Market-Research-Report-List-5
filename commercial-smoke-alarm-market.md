<p><p>The commercial smoke alarm market consists of three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and dual sensor smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms are designed to quickly detect smoldering fires, while ionization smoke alarms are more responsive to fast-flaming fires. Dual sensor smoke alarms utilize both photoelectric and ionization technologies to provide comprehensive smoke detection. Each type of smoke alarm has its own advantages and is suitable for different fire scenarios in commercial settings.</p></p>
