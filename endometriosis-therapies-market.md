<p><p>Endometriosis therapies encompass various treatment options aimed at managing the symptoms of endometriosis. These include hormonal contraceptives, which help regulate menstrual cycles and reduce pain. Gonadotropin-releasing hormone (Gn-RH) agonists work by suppressing estrogen production, while progestin therapy helps inhibit growth of endometrial tissue. Aromatase inhibitors reduce estrogen levels in the body, which can alleviate symptoms of endometriosis. These therapies are commonly used to alleviate pain, heavy bleeding, and infertility associated with endometriosis.</p></p>
