<p><p>Three-dimensional woven fabric market is divided into two main types: hollow fabric and solid fabric. </p><p>Hollow fabric is characterized by its unique structure with hollow spaces within the fabric, providing lightweight and breathable properties. It is commonly used in applications where weight reduction and insulation are important factors. </p><p>On the other hand, solid fabric is made of tightly woven fibers without any hollow spaces, providing durability and strength. Solid fabric is often used in applications where high tensile strength and abrasion resistance are required.</p></p>
