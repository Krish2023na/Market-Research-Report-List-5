<p><p>Step Profilers are devices used to measure the surface roughness of a material. There are three main market types of Step Profilers: Inductive, Piezo, and Photoelectric. Inductive Step Profilers use electrical signals to measure surface roughness. Piezo Step Profilers use piezoelectric crystals to generate signals for measurement. Photoelectric Step Profilers use light beams to measure surface roughness. Each type of Step Profiler has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for different industries and materials.</p></p>
