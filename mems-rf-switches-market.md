<p><p>MEMS RF switches are categorized into two main types: Shunt Switches and Series Switches. Shunt switches are known for their high isolation and low insertion loss, making them ideal for applications requiring high performance. On the other hand, series switches are suitable for high-power applications due to their ability to handle higher power levels. Both types of MEMS RF switches are widely used in various industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive, where reliable and efficient RF signal switching is essential.</p></p>
