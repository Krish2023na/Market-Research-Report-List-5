<p><p>Piston filling machines come in two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic piston filling machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production lines. On the other hand, semi-automatic piston filling machines require some manual operation, such as placing containers and activating the machine, but are more cost-effective for smaller production runs. Both types of machines are efficient in accurately filling liquids, creams, and other products into containers of various sizes.</p></p>
