<p><p>Glutathione Agarose Resin is available in two main types: Glutathione Reduced and Glutathione Oxidized. Glutathione Reduced resin contains reduced glutathione, which has a free thiol group that can bind to target proteins for purification. On the other hand, Glutathione Oxidized resin contains oxidized glutathione, which is used for capturing proteins that contain disulfide bonds. Both types of resins are commonly used in affinity chromatography for the purification of proteins or peptides that specifically interact with glutathione.</p></p>
