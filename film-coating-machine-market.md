<p><p>Film coating machines are classified into two main types: Evaporation Coating Equipment and Sputter Coating Equipment. Evaporation Coating Equipment involves heating a material until it evaporates and then condenses onto the substrate, creating a thin film. Sputter Coating Equipment uses a plasma to bombard a target material, causing atoms to be ejected and deposited onto the substrate. Both types of equipment are commonly used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging for applications such as decorative coatings, anti-reflective coatings, and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
