<p><p>The underground utility locator market includes various types of technologies used to detect underground utilities. Acoustic locating uses sound waves to locate underground objects. Ground penetrating radar sends radar pulses into the ground to identify buried utilities. Magnetic locators detect metal objects underground. Wires and markers are used to mark the location of utilities. Other technologies may also be used for underground utility location.</p></p>
