<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Lime Mortars</li><li>Non-hydraulic Lime Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lime Mortar market consists of two main types - Hydraulic Lime Mortars and Non-hydraulic Lime Mortars. Hydraulic Lime Mortars are known for their ability to set and harden underwater, making them suitable for damp environments. On the other hand, Non-hydraulic Lime Mortars require exposure to air to carbonate and harden. Both types have their own advantages and applications in the construction industry, with Hydraulic Lime Mortars being preferred for areas with high moisture levels and Non-hydraulic Lime Mortars for dry environments.</p></p>
