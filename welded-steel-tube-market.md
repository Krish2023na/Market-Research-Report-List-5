<p><strong>The Welded Steel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Seam Welded Pipe</li><li>Spiral Welded Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welded steel tube market consists of two main types: straight seam welded pipe and spiral welded pipe. Straight seam welded pipe is made by bending a steel plate and welding the seam in a straight line, making it suitable for high-pressure applications. Spiral welded pipe is made by rolling a steel plate into a cylindrical shape and welding the seam in a spiral pattern, making it ideal for transporting fluids and gases. Both types offer different benefits depending on the specific application.</p></p>
