<p><p>A wind turbine gearbox is a key component of a wind turbine system that helps in transferring the power generated by the wind into electrical energy. The market growth is being driven by the increasing demand for renewable energy sources, government initiatives to promote wind energy, and technological advancements in gearbox design and manufacturing. These trends are expected to further drive the growth of the wind turbine gearbox market in the coming years.</p></p>
