<p><p>Image sensors are electronic devices used to convert an optical image into an electronic signal. The two main types dominating the market are CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) image sensors. CMOS sensors are more cost-effective and power-efficient, making them popular for consumer electronics like smartphones. On the other hand, CCD sensors offer better image quality and are preferred in high-end applications such as professional photography and medical imaging. Other image sensor markets include InGaAs image sensors for infrared imaging and X-ray sensors for medical and industrial applications.</p></p>
