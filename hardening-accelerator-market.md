<p><p>Hardening accelerators are used in construction to speed up the setting and hardening of concrete. Two common types of hardening accelerators are liquid hardening accelerators and dry powder hardening accelerators. Liquid hardening accelerators are in liquid form and are typically added directly to the concrete mix. Dry powder hardening accelerators are in powder form and are usually added to the concrete mix as a dry admixture. Both types of accelerators help improve the early strength development and overall durability of concrete structures.</p></p>
