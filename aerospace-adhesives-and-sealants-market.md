<p><p>Aerospace adhesives and sealants market can be broadly categorized into two types: reactive adhesives and non-reactive adhesives. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction to bond materials together, resulting in a strong and durable bond. On the other hand, non-reactive adhesives do not require a chemical reaction and provide instant bonding. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in aerospace applications, providing solutions for bonding and sealing components in aircraft structures, engines, and other critical aerospace systems.</p></p>
