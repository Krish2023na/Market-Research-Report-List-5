<p><p>Infrared Photoelectric Sensor Market Types can be broadly classified as Active and Passive. Active sensors emit infrared light and detect the reflection back, making them suitable for detecting objects. Passive sensors, on the other hand, do not emit any light and instead detect the infrared radiation emitted by objects, making them ideal for temperature sensing applications. Both types of sensors have their own set of advantages and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
