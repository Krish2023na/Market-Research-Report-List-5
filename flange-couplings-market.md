<p><p>Flange couplings are commonly categorized into two main types: rigid flange couplings and flexible flange couplings. Rigid flange couplings are designed to provide a secure and rigid connection between two shafts, allowing for efficient power transmission. On the other hand, flexible flange couplings are designed to accommodate misalignment between shafts, reducing vibration and wear on equipment. Both types of flange couplings play a crucial role in various industries where precise and reliable shaft coupling is required.</p></p>
