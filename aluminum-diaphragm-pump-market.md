<p><p>The Aluminum Diaphragm Pump market includes two main types: Single Acting and Double Acting pumps. Single Acting pumps use air pressure to push the diaphragm in one direction, creating suction and discharge strokes. Double Acting pumps use air pressure to create suction and discharge strokes in both directions, increasing efficiency and output. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industrial and chemical processing applications due to their reliable and versatile diaphragm design.</p></p>
