<p><p>AI chips market consists of different types: GPU, ASIC, FPGA, CPU. Graphic Processing Units (GPU) are specialized for parallel processing, ideal for deep learning. Application-Specific Integrated Circuits (ASIC) are customized for specific AI workloads, offering high efficiency. Field-Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGA) provide flexibility to be reprogrammed for different tasks. Central Processing Units (CPU) are general-purpose chips used for various functions but are less efficient for AI tasks. Each type has its own strengths and is suited for different AI applications.</p></p>
