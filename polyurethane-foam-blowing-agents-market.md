<p><p>Polyurethane foam blowing agents are chemicals used to create gas bubbles in the foam, resulting in its light and insulating properties. There are four main types of blowing agents: Hydrocarbons (HC), Hydroflurocarbon (HFC), Hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFC), and Hydrofluorocarbon (HFO). HC blowing agents are environmentally friendly but flammable. HFC blowing agents have low ozone depletion potential. HCFC blowing agents have medium ozone depletion potential and are being phased out. HFO blowing agents are the most environmentally friendly option with zero ozone depletion potential.</p></p>
