Vertical Needle/Tip Probe Cards utilize vertical needles or tips for contacting integrated circuits. Thin Film MLO Probe Cards are made with thin film technology and MLO (Multi Layer Organic) substrates. Membrane Probe Cards feature a flexible membrane that conforms to the device under test.
