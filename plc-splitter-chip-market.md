<p><strong>The PLC Splitter Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1xN</li><li>2xN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PLC splitter chip market can be categorized into two main types based on the number of output ports: 1xN and 2xN. In a 1xN market, the splitter chip has one input port and multiple output ports, allowing one signal to be split into multiple signals. On the other hand, in a 2xN market, the splitter chip has two input ports and multiple output ports, enabling two different signals to be split into multiple signals. Both types are commonly used in fiber optic networks for efficient signal distribution.</p></p>
