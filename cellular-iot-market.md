<p><p>The Cellular IoT market comprises various technologies such as 2G, 3G, 4G, LTE-M, NB-LTE-M, NB-IoT, and 5G. 2G technology provides basic connectivity, while 3G offers faster data speeds. 4G and LTE-M provide enhanced performance and efficiency, suitable for demanding IoT applications. The upcoming 5G network promises ultra-low latency, high data speeds, and massive device connectivity, making it ideal for advanced IoT solutions. Each technology caters to different IoT use cases and requirements.</p></p>
