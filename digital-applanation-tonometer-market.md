<p><p>A digital applanation tonometer is a type of instrument used to measure intraocular pressure in the eye, which is important for diagnosing and monitoring conditions such as glaucoma. This device works by flattening a small area of the cornea and measuring the force required to do so. Digital applanation tonometers offer advantages such as improved accuracy and ease of use compared to traditional tonometers.</p><p>The Digital Applanation Tonometer Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
