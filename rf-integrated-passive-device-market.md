<p><p>RF integrated passive devices (IPDs) are a type of electronic components that are integrated into a single chip for the purpose of radio-frequency (RF) applications. These devices typically consist of passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors that are integrated into a single package to reduce size, cost, and complexity of RF circuits.</p><p>The RF integrated passive device market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for compact and high-performance RF components in the telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive industries. The adoption of 5G technology is also contributing to the growth of the RF IPD market, as 5G networks require advanced RF components for improved performance.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the RF integrated passive device market is the development of advanced packaging technologies such as system-in-package (SiP) and wafer-level packaging (WLP). These technologies enable the integration of multiple RF components into a single package, further reducing the size and cost of RF circuits. Additionally, the increasing demand for RF IPDs with higher integration levels and improved performance is driving innovations in the market.</p></p>
