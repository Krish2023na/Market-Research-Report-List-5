<p><p>Thermal energy flow meters are utilized for measuring the flow rate of fluids by monitoring the amount of heat transferred. There are three main types of thermal energy flow meters: insertion, portable, and inline. Insertion flow meters are installed directly into the pipeline to measure flow rates. Portable flow meters are handheld devices that can be easily transported for on-the-go measurements. Inline flow meters are permanently installed within the pipeline for continuous monitoring of flow rates. Each type caters to different needs and applications within various industries.</p></p>
