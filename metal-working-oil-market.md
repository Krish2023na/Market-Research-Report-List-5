<p><strong>The Metal Working Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cutting Processing Oil</li><li>Molding Processing Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metalworking oil market is divided into two main types: cutting processing oil and molding processing oil. Cutting processing oil is used during cutting, milling, drilling, and grinding operations to reduce friction and heat generation, improve tool life, and provide better surface finish. On the other hand, molding processing oil is used in processes like forging, extrusion, and stamping to lubricate the dies and reduce wear and tear. Both types of oils play a crucial role in improving efficiency and productivity in metalworking operations.</p></p>
