<p><p>Cryogenic check valves are available in various types to suit different applications. Manual type valves are operated manually by turning a wheel or lever. Electric type valves are controlled by an electric signal, offering more precise and automated operation. Pneumatic type valves use compressed air to control the flow of cryogenic fluids. Hydraulic type valves utilize hydraulic pressure to regulate flow. Each type of valve offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the cryogenic system.</p></p>
