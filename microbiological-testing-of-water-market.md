Legionella testing is focused on preventing Legionnaires' Disease, while Coliform testing indicates the presence of fecal contamination. Salmonella and Vibrio testing targets specific bacteria strains, and Clostridium testing detects bacteria that may cause foodborne illnesses. Other tests target various bacteria, protozoa, and viruses that can be harmful to human health.</p></p>
