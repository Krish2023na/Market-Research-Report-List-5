<p><p>Photovoltaic laminated glass interlayers play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and durability of solar panels. The market types for these interlayers include PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral), EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate), SGP (SentryGlas Plus), and other specialized materials. PVB interlayers are known for their excellent adhesion properties, while EVA interlayers offer better light transmission. SGP interlayers are preferred for their superior strength and toughness. Other market materials can include ionoplast and thermoplastic interlayers, each offering unique benefits for photovoltaic applications.</p></p>
