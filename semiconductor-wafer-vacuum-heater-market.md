<p><p>Single Zone Vacuum Heater refers to a semiconductor wafer vacuum heater that has a single heating zone, providing uniform heating across the entire wafer surface. On the other hand, Multi Zone Vacuum Heater features multiple heating zones, allowing for more precise control over the temperature distribution on the wafer. Single Zone Vacuum Heaters are usually more cost-effective and simpler to operate, while Multi Zone Vacuum Heaters are preferred for applications requiring different temperature profiles on the wafer. Both types cater to different needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
