<p><p>Dry Topping Dispenser market offers two main types: Wall-Mount and Countertop dispensers. Wall-Mount dispensers are installed directly on the wall, saving valuable countertop space and providing a sleek, modern look. They are ideal for establishments with limited space. On the other hand, Countertop dispensers are placed on top of a surface and are easier to access for customers. They offer a wider range of customization options and are commonly used in self-serve buffets or ice cream parlors. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the foodservice industry.</p></p>
