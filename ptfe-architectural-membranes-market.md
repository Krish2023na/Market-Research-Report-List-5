<p><strong>The PTFE Architectural Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE Architectural Membranes come in two types: Hydrophobic and Hydrophilic. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes are water repellent, making them ideal for applications where water resistance is crucial, such as roofing. On the other hand, hydrophilic PTFE membranes are designed to attract and hold water, making them suitable for applications where water absorption is desired, such as in landscaping. Both types offer unique properties that cater to different architectural needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
