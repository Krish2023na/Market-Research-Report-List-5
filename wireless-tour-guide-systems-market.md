<p><p>Wireless tour guide systems market is classified into two main types based on signal transmission technology: Digital Signal Transmission and Analog Signal Transmission. Digital signal transmission systems offer higher quality audio, improved signal clarity, and better resistance to interference compared to analog systems. Analog signal transmission systems, on the other hand, are simpler and more cost-effective but may experience more audio distortion and interference. The choice between these two types depends on the specific needs and budget of the user.</p></p>
