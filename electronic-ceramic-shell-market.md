<p><ul><li>With-pin</li><li>Without-pin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Ceramic Shell market can be categorized into two main types: With-pin and Without-pin. With-pin shells have metal pins attached to them, which are used for soldering components onto circuit boards. Without-pin shells, on the other hand, do not have these pins and are typically used for surface mounting components directly onto the board. Both types offer different advantages and are suitable for various applications depending on the specific requirements of the electronic device being manufactured.</p></p>
