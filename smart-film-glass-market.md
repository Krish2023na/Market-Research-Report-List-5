<p><strong>The Smart Film Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Film Glass Market comprises three major types - Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic Smart Glass changes its tint when voltage is applied, controlling light and heat transmission. SPD Smart Glass uses suspended particle technology to adjust its opacity when a small electrical current is applied. PDLC Smart Glass contains a layer of liquid crystal molecules that align and become transparent when an electric current is passed through them, and opaque when the current is turned off.</p></p>
