<p><p>Air to Air Enthalpy Wheels market types include Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation Rotors utilize the condensation of water vapor to exchange heat between air streams, while Hygroscopic Rotors absorb and release moisture to achieve heat exchange. Sorption Rotors use a desiccant material to remove moisture from the air stream. Each type of rotor is designed to effectively transfer heat and humidity between incoming and outgoing air streams in HVAC systems, ensuring efficient and comfortable indoor environments.</p></p>
