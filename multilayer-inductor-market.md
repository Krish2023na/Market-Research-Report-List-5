<p><p>The Multilayer Inductor market consists of two main types based on the technology used for installation: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT involves mounting components directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, providing smaller size and higher density. On the other hand, THT involves soldering components through holes in the board, offering better mechanical stability. Both technologies serve different applications and have their own advantages, catering to the diverse needs of the electronics industry.</p></p>
