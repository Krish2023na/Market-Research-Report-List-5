<p><p>Self-unloading systems are used in industries to quickly and efficiently unload bulk materials from vessels. There are four main types of self-unloading systems: pneumatic, grab rope trolley, vertical screw, and elevator bucket chain. </p><p>Pneumatic systems use air pressure to move materials through pipes. Grab rope trolley systems utilize a crane with a grab bucket to unload materials. Vertical screw systems have a rotating screw that moves materials vertically. Elevator bucket chain systems use a series of buckets attached to a chain to lift and unload materials.</p></p>
