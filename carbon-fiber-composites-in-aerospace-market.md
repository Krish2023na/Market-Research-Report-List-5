<p><p>The carbon fiber composites in aerospace market can be categorized into two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting composites are materials that harden irreversibly when heated, making them ideal for structural components that require high strength and stiffness. In contrast, thermoplastic composites can be reheated and reshaped multiple times, offering greater flexibility in manufacturing and repair processes. Both types of carbon fiber composites are commonly used in the aerospace industry to reduce weight, improve fuel efficiency, and enhance overall performance of aircraft.</p></p>
