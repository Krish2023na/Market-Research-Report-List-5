<p><p>Thin-film power inductors are commonly used in electronics for energy storage and filtering applications. The two main market types for thin-film power inductors are Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology. SMT inductors are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them suitable for automated assembly processes. Through Hole Technology inductors are inserted into holes on the board and soldered in place manually. Both market types provide different options for integrating inductors into electronic devices based on specific assembly requirements.</p></p>
