<p><p>Ball Segment Valves are available in two main market types: Static Seal and Inflatable Seal. </p><p>Static Seal Ball Segment Valves use a fixed seal to prevent leakage between the ball segment and the valve seat. This type is commonly used in applications where a permanent seal is needed.</p><p>In contrast, Inflatable Seal Ball Segment Valves utilize an inflatable seal to create a tight seal between the ball segment and the valve seat. This type is ideal for applications where frequent maintenance or adjustments are required.</p></p>
