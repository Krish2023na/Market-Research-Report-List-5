<p><strong>The SWIR Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Products</li><li>Line Scan Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SWIR equipment market consists of two main types of products: Area Scan and Line Scan. Area scan products capture an entire image at once, making them suitable for still or slow-moving subjects. Line scan products capture images line by line, making them ideal for fast-moving subjects or continuous monitoring. Area scan products are commonly used in surveillance, industrial inspection, and medical imaging, while line scan products are commonly used in machine vision, sorting, and quality control applications.</p></p>
