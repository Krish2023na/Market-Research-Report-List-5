<p><p>Hospital Room Environmental Monitors come in two main types, fixed and portable. Fixed monitors are stationary devices typically mounted on the wall or ceiling to continuously monitor air quality, temperature, and humidity in a specific area. Portable monitors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily moved and used to spot-check environmental conditions in various locations throughout a hospital room. Both types serve the important purpose of ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
