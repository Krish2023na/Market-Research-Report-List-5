<p><p>The mobile communication infrastructure market is divided into several key types of technology including Radio Access Networks, Base Transceiver Stations, Mobile Softswitching, Packet Core Equipment, and E-UTRAN Macrocells. Radio Access Networks are responsible for connecting mobile devices to the network. Base Transceiver Stations transmit and receive signals to and from mobile devices. Mobile Softswitching manages the connection between the radio network and the core network. Packet Core Equipment handles data traffic within the network. E-UTRAN Macrocells provide coverage over a wide area for mobile communication services.</p></p>
