<p><p>Ultrafiltration polymer membrane market can be divided into two types: inorganic membrane and organic membrane. Inorganic membranes are made from materials like ceramic or metal, offering high chemical and thermal stability. On the other hand, organic membranes are made from polymers like polyethylene or polypropylene, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types of membranes are widely used in various industries for applications such as water purification, wastewater treatment, and protein separation due to their ability to selectively filter molecules based on size and shape.</p></p>
