<p><p>Cold insulation is commonly categorized into three main types: fiberglass, polyurethane, and polyisocyanurate foam, and polystyrene foam. Fiberglass insulation is cost-effective and provides good thermal performance. Polyurethane and polyisocyanurate foam insulation are known for their excellent insulating properties and durability. Polystyrene foam insulation is lightweight and moisture-resistant. Each type of insulation has its own advantages and is used in various applications to provide thermal insulation for cold storage facilities, refrigeration units, and other cold environments.</p></p>
