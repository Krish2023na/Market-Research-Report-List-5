<p><p>Direct thermal printing film is available in two main types - white thermal printing film and transparent thermal printing film. White thermal printing film is coated with a layer of white material that provides a solid background for printing clear, sharp images and text. Transparent thermal printing film, on the other hand, allows for see-through visibility, making it ideal for applications where the underlying surface needs to remain visible. Both types of films are widely used in various industries for labeling, packaging, and printing applications.</p></p>
