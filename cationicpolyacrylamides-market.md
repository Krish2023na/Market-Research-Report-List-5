<p><p>Cationic polyacrylamides are used in two main forms in the market: solid particles and emulsions. Solid particle cationic polyacrylamides are in the form of dry powder or granules and are primarily used in wastewater treatment applications. On the other hand, cationic polyacrylamide emulsions are liquid solutions that are diluted with water before application. These emulsions are commonly used in industries such as papermaking, mining, and oil and gas for their flocculating and thickening properties.</p></p>
