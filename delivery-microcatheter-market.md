<p><strong>The Delivery Microcatheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen Microcatheters</li><li>Dual-Lumen Microcatheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Delivery microcatheters are categorized into two main types: single-lumen microcatheters and dual-lumen microcatheters. Single-lumen microcatheters consist of a single channel for delivering fluids or medications, making them suitable for simple procedures. On the other hand, dual-lumen microcatheters have two channels which allow for more complex procedures such as simultaneous infusion and aspiration or contrast injection. Each type of microcatheter has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the procedure.</p></p>
