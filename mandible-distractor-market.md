<p><p>Mandible distractors used for jaw reconstruction come in two main types: multi-vector distraction and bi-directional distraction. Multi-vector distraction devices allow for movement in multiple directions to better align the jaw. Bi-directional distraction devices mainly focus on moving the jaw forward or backward. Both types offer different capabilities and options for surgeons in mandible reconstruction surgeries, catering to specific patient needs and desired outcomes. Each type of distractor has its own unique market within the overall mandible distractor market.</p></p>
