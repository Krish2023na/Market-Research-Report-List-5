<p><p>Keypad HMI displays are categorized into two main types - membrane keypads and mechanical keypads. Membrane keypads are made of a thin, flexible layer that is pressed to make contact with a circuit underneath, providing a sleek and modern design. Mechanical keypads, on the other hand, have physical buttons that require more force to press but offer a tactile feedback. Both types of keypads are commonly used in various industrial applications for controlling and monitoring machinery and equipment.</p></p>
