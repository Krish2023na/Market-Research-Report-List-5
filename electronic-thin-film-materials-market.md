High Purity Metal Material refers to metals that have been refined to extremely high levels of purity for use in thin film applications. Sputtering Target is a material used in the process of sputtering, where atoms are ejected from a target material and deposited onto a substrate to create thin films. Evaporation Film Material is used in the evaporation process to create thin films on surfaces.</p></p>
