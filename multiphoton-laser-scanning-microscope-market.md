<p><p>Multiphoton laser scanning microscopes are available in three main types based on their design and configuration - upright microscope system, gantry microscope system, and inverted microscope system. </p><p>Upright microscope systems are commonly used for large samples or tissues that require vertical imaging, while gantry microscope systems are ideal for imaging moving samples or processes. </p><p>Inverted microscope systems are best suited for imaging live cells or tissues in culture dishes. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the research or imaging application.</p></p>
