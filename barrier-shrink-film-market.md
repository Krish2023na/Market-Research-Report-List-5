<p><p>Barrier shrink film is categorized into four types based on barrier properties: Low, Medium, High, and Ultra-high market. Low barrier shrink film provides basic protection against external elements. Medium barrier film offers enhanced protection against moisture and oxygen. High barrier film provides superior protection against oxygen, moisture, and other external factors, making it ideal for sensitive products. Ultra-high barrier film offers the highest level of protection, ensuring maximum shelf life and freshness for products that require long-term preservation. Each type caters to different requirements of the packaging industry.</p></p>
