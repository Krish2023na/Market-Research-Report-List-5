<p><p>Chlorine Dioxide Generation Systems Market Types include the Acid-Chlorite Method and Chlorine-Chlorite Method. The Acid-Chlorite Method involves the reaction of sodium chlorite with hydrochloric or sulfuric acid to produce chlorine dioxide. On the other hand, the Chlorine-Chlorite Method utilizes the reaction of chlorine gas with sodium chlorite to generate chlorine dioxide. Both methods are commonly used in industries such as water treatment, pulp and paper, and oil and gas for disinfection and bleaching purposes.</p></p>
