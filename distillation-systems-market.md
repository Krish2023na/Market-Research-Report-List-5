<p><p>Distillation systems market includes several types such as fractional, steam, vacuum, multiple-effect (MED), and others. Fractional distillation separates components based on their boiling points using a column with several trays. Steam distillation is used for heat-sensitive materials by introducing steam into the mixture. Vacuum distillation lowers the boiling point by reducing the pressure. Multiple-effect distillation involves multiple stages to increase efficiency. Other types may include simple distillation setups or unique techniques tailored to specific industries or applications.</p></p>
