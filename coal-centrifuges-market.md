<p><p>Coal centrifuges are specialized equipment used in the coal industry to separate coal particles based on their sizes. The two main types of coal centrifuges are coarse coal centrifuges and fine coal centrifuges. Coarse coal centrifuges are designed to handle larger coal particles, typically in the size range of 0-50mm, while fine coal centrifuges are used for smaller particles, usually in the range of 0-6mm. Each type of centrifuge is tailored to efficiently separate and dewater coal particles of specific sizes.</p></p>
