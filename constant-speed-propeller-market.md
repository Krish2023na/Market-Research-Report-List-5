<p><p>Constant Speed Propeller market can be categorized into two main types: Hydraulic Propellers and Electric Propellers. Hydraulic propellers use hydraulic fluid to adjust the pitch of the propeller blades, allowing for precise control of engine speed. On the other hand, Electric Propellers use electric motors to adjust the pitch of the blades, offering a more efficient and reliable alternative to hydraulic systems. Both types offer benefits in terms of performance and fuel efficiency, catering to different needs and preferences in the aviation industry.</p></p>
