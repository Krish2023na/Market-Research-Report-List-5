<p><p>Refractory bauxite market types refer to the different grades of bauxite based on their aluminum oxide (Al2O3) content. Bauxite with an Al2O3 content below 85% is classified as low-grade, while bauxite with an Al2O3 content above 85% is considered high-grade. Low-grade refractory bauxite is typically used in applications where the purity requirement is not as stringent, while high-grade bauxite is preferred for applications that require higher levels of alumina content and purity, such as in the production of refractory bricks.</p></p>
