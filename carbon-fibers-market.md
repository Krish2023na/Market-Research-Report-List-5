<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber refers to small bundles of carbon fibers twisted together. It is commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and sporting goods due to its high strength-to-weight ratio. On the other hand, Large-Tow Carbon Fiber consists of larger bundles of carbon fibers, providing higher efficiency in manufacturing processes and cost-effectiveness. This type of carbon fiber is ideal for applications requiring high stiffness and lightweight properties, such as wind energy and marine industries.</p></p>
