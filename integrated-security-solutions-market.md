<p><p>The Integrated Security Solutions market encompasses three main types: Closed Circuit Surveillance (CCTV), Perimeter Security (PS), and Access Control Security (ACS). CCTV systems use video cameras to monitor and record activities within a specific area. Perimeter Security focuses on securing the boundaries of a property using sensors, barriers, and alarms. Access Control Security manages and restricts entry to buildings or areas through keycards, biometric scanners, or passwords. These solutions work together to provide comprehensive security measures for businesses and residential properties.</p></p>
