<p><strong>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-hole Technology</li><li>Surface-mount Technology</li><li>Plug-in Technology</li><li>Solderless Stacking Mezzanine Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial board-to-board connectors come in various types based on the technology used for their connection. Through-hole technology involves inserting pins into pre-drilled holes on the circuit board. Surface-mount technology connects the connectors directly to the surface of the board. Plug-in technology uses plug-style connectors for easy installation. Solderless stacking mezzanine technology allows for connectors to be stacked vertically without the need for soldering, providing a versatile and modular solution for connecting boards in industrial applications.</p></p>
