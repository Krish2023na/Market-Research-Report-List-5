<p><p>Green-roofs are classified into three main types based on their depth and maintenance requirements. Extensive green-roofs are shallow, require minimal maintenance, and typically consist of low-growing vegetation. Semi-intensive green-roofs are deeper than extensive green-roofs, require some maintenance, and can support a wider variety of plant species. Intensive green-roofs are the deepest, require the most maintenance, and can support trees, shrubs, and even small gardens. Each type offers different benefits and challenges depending on the desired aesthetic and functionality of the green-roof.</p></p>
