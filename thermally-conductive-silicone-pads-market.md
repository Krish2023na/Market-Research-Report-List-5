<p><p>Thermally conductive silicone pads are widely categorized into two main market types: Ordinary and Strong Adhesive. Ordinary silicone pads provide basic thermal conductivity for applications where strong adhesion is not a priority. On the other hand, Strong Adhesive silicone pads are designed for applications that require both high thermal conductivity and strong bonding capabilities. These pads are ideal for use in electronic devices, LED lighting, automotive components, and other heat-sensitive applications where a secure bond is essential for optimal performance.</p></p>
