<p><p>Soil remediation technology market can be categorized into three main types based on the methods used. Biological based remediation involves the use of microorganisms to break down contaminants in the soil. Physical remediation includes techniques like excavation, soil vapor extraction, and thermal treatment to remove or neutralize pollutants. Chemical remediation utilizes chemicals to treat the soil and remove contaminants. Each type of remediation method has its own advantages and limitations, and the choice of method depends on the specific soil contamination and site conditions.</p></p>
