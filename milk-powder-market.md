Whole Milk Powder is produced by evaporating water from fresh pasteurized whole milk and then drying it. It contains the natural cream content of whole milk and is often used in baking, beverage mixes, and confectionery products. Both types of milk powder are versatile ingredients that have a long shelf life and are popular in the food industry.</p></p>
