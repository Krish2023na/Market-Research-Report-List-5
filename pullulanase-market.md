<p><p>Pullulanase is an enzyme that plays a crucial role in breaking down starch molecules. There are two main types of pullulanase in the market - debranching enzyme and amylopullulanase. Debranching enzyme specifically targets the side chains of starch molecules, while amylopullulanase acts on both straight and branched starch molecules. These enzymes are widely used in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biofuels production to improve the efficiency of starch processing and increase product yield.</p></p>
