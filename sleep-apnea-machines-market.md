<p><ul><li>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP)</li><li>Bi-Level Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP)</li><li>Automatic Airway Pressure (APAP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sleep apnea machines market types include Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), Bi-Level Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP), and Automatic Airway Pressure (APAP). CPAP machines deliver a constant level of air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. BiPAP machines offer two different pressures: higher pressure during inhalation and lower pressure during exhalation. APAP machines automatically adjust the air pressure based on the patient's breathing patterns. These machines help individuals with sleep apnea breathe more easily and improve their quality of sleep.</p></p>
