<p><p>Solvent-grade mixed xylenes refer to a group of hydrocarbon chemicals primarily used as solvents in various industrial applications. These mixed xylenes are classified into two main types: Solvent Grade and Isomer Grade. Solvent Grade mixed xylenes are a mixture of all three isomers of xylene: ortho, meta, and para. On the other hand, Isomer Grade mixed xylenes are specifically tailored to contain a higher percentage of one particular isomer, usually para-xylene, for more specific applications such as the production of purified terephthalic acid for polyester production.</p></p>
