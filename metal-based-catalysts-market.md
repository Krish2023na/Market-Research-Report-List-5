<p><p>Metal Based Catalysts are classified into two major types: Precious Metal Based Catalyst and Non-precious Metal Based Catalyst. Precious Metal Based Catalysts utilize expensive metals like platinum, palladium, and gold, providing high catalytic activity and efficiency. On the other hand, Non-precious Metal Based Catalysts use more abundant and cost-effective metals like nickel, iron, and cobalt. While Precious Metal Based Catalysts are favored for their superior performance, Non-precious Metal Based Catalysts offer a more economical alternative without compromising on catalytic activity.</p></p>
