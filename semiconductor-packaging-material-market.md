<p><p>Semiconductor packaging materials contain various types such as organic substrates, bonding wires, encapsulation resins, ceramic packages, solder balls, wafer level packaging dielectrics, and others. Organic substrates are used as the base for semiconductor packages, while bonding wires transmit signals within the package. Encapsulation resins protect the semiconductor device from external elements. Ceramic packages offer high thermal conductivity. Solder balls provide electrical connections. Wafer level packaging dielectrics insulate and protect the semiconductor device. Other materials include underfill materials, leadframes, and thermal interface materials.</p></p>
