<p><ul><li>NOACs</li><li>Heparin</li><li>Warfarin</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antithrombotic Treatment Market includes various types such as NOACs (Novel Oral Anticoagulants), Heparin, Warfarin, and Other anticoagulant drugs. NOACs are relatively newer medications that work by inhibiting specific clotting factors in the blood. Heparin is a rapidly acting anticoagulant commonly used in hospital settings. Warfarin is an older anticoagulant that works by interfering with Vitamin K-dependent clotting factors. Other anticoagulants may include direct thrombin inhibitors or factor Xa inhibitors. Each type of anticoagulant has its own advantages and limitations in managing thrombotic disorders.</p></p>
