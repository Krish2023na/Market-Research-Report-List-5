<p><p>X-ray Inspection Systems market includes Digital Radiography (DR) Type and Computed Tomography (CT) Type systems. Digital Radiography (DR) Type uses digital sensors to capture X-ray images, providing high-quality images with reduced radiation exposure. Computed Tomography (CT) Type systems use a series of X-ray images taken from different angles to create detailed 3D images of the inspected object. Both types of systems are essential in industries such as healthcare, automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for non-destructive testing and quality control purposes.</p></p>
