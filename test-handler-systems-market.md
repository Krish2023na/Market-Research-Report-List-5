<p><strong>The Test Handler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity-Feed Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Test handler systems are devices used in semiconductor testing to automate the process of handling and testing semiconductor devices. Gravity-feed handlers rely on gravity to feed devices into the testing area. Turret handlers use a rotating turret to pick up and place devices for testing. Pick-and-place handlers use a robotic arm to pick up and place devices. Each type of handler system has its own advantages and can be chosen based on specific testing requirements and production needs.</p></p>
