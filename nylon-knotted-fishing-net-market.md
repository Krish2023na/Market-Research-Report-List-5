<p><p>Nylon knotted fishing nets are categorized into two main market types: Depthway and Lengthway. Depthway refers to fishing nets that are designed to catch fish vertically, extending deep into the water. These nets are typically used for species that swim at varying depths. Lengthway fishing nets, on the other hand, are designed to catch fish horizontally, covering a larger surface area. These nets are commonly used for species that swim in schools or in a linear fashion. Each market type serves specific purposes based on the fishing techniques and target species.</p></p>
