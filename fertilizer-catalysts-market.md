<p><p>Fertilizer Catalysts market can be classified into two main types - Base Metal Catalysts and Precious Metal Catalysts. Base Metal Catalysts are typically made from metals like nickel, copper, or iron and are more cost-effective but have lower catalytic efficiency compared to Precious Metal Catalysts. On the other hand, Precious Metal Catalysts are made from metals like platinum, palladium, or rhodium and are more efficient but also more expensive. Both types play a crucial role in increasing the efficiency of fertilizer production processes.</p></p>
