<p><p>Festoon cable market consists of two main types: flat and round cables. Flat cables are made up of multiple insulated conductors laid parallel to each other and are typically used in applications where space is limited. On the other hand, round cables have a circular cross-section and are more flexible, making them suitable for applications that require frequent bending or movement. Both types of cables are used for suspending and supplying power to mobile equipment in various industries such as automotive, construction, and material handling.</p></p>
