<p><p>Patrol ACVs can be divided into two main types: Skirted Air-cushion Vehicles and Sidewall Air-cushion Vehicles. Skirted ACVs have a flexible skirt that surrounds the vehicle, creating an air cushion that lifts it off the ground. This design allows for smooth navigation over various terrains. On the other hand, Sidewall ACVs have inflatable sidewalls that provide stability and buoyancy on water. Both types are commonly used for patrol missions in coastal regions and marshy areas due to their ability to traverse challenging environments efficiently.</p></p>
