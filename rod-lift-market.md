<p><strong>The Rod Lift Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam</li><li>Non-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rod lift market can be categorized into two main types: Beam and Non-beam systems. Beam lift systems typically utilize a surface-mounted beam that supports the rod string and aids in the vertical movement of the pump. On the other hand, non-beam lift systems do not rely on a beam for support and instead use other means to lift the pump. Both types of systems have their own advantages and disadvantages, with beam systems often being more stable and easier to maintain, while non-beam systems may provide more flexibility in certain situations.</p></p>
