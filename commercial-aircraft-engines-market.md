<p><p>Commercial aircraft engines can be categorized into two main types: turbojet engines and gas turbine engines. Turbojet engines operate by taking in air, compressing it, mixing it with fuel, igniting the mixture, and then expelling it through a nozzle to generate thrust. Gas turbine engines, on the other hand, use a series of turbines powered by hot gases produced by burning fuel to generate thrust. Both types of engines play a crucial role in powering commercial aircraft and ensuring safe and efficient flights.</p></p>
