<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave Resonator market is divided into two main types - DIP (Dual In-line Package) and Surface Mount. DIP resonators are typically used for through-hole mounting on a PCB, providing stability and ease of installation. On the other hand, Surface Mount resonators are designed for surface mounting directly onto the PCB, saving space and allowing for automated assembly processes. Both types of resonators offer high precision frequency control and are commonly used in various applications such as telecommunication, consumer electronics, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
