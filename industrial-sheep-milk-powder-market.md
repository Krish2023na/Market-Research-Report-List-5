<p><p>Industrial Sheep Milk Powder comes in two main types: Skimmed and Full Cream. Skimmed milk powder is produced by removing most of the fat content from the milk, resulting in a lower fat percentage and a higher protein content. Full cream milk powder, on the other hand, contains all of the fat from the milk, giving it a creamier texture and richer taste. Both types of milk powder are popular in the market for various industrial applications, including food processing and infant formula production.</p></p>
