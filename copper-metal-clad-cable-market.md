<p><p>Copper Metal-Clad Cable market can be categorized into two types: Armored and Non-Armored. Armored cables are protected with an additional layer of metal, such as aluminum or steel, to provide extra strength and durability, making them ideal for harsh environments and areas prone to physical damage. Non-Armored cables do not have this extra layer of protection, making them more flexible and lightweight, suitable for indoor installations where physical damage is less of a concern. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the installation.</p></p>
