<p><ul><li>Gloss Method</li><li>Gravimetric Method</li><li>Haze Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fogging tester is used to measure the amount of volatile substances emitted from materials under certain conditions. The Gloss Method measures the reduction in gloss caused by fogging, the Gravimetric Method measures the weight gain of a collector due to fogging, and the Haze Method measures the increase in haze caused by fogging. These different market types cater to manufacturers and industries looking to ensure product quality and comply with regulations related to volatile emissions.</p></p>
