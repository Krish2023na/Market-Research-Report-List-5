<p><p>The vehicle lighting tool market consists of three main types: Halogen lighting tools, HID lighting tools, and LED lighting tools. Halogen lighting tools are known for their affordability and easy replacement, but they are less energy-efficient compared to HID and LED lighting tools. HID lighting tools provide bright and long-lasting illumination, making them popular for high-performance vehicles. LED lighting tools are the most energy-efficient and long-lasting option, offering a wide range of color options and customization possibilities. Each type of lighting tool caters to different preferences and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
